May I?
======

Asking if an action is ok can be approached in a couple of different ways.
Both use the ーて form.

Example
-------

- "May I borrow your eraser?"
- 「消しゴムを借**りてもいい**ですか。」

The second method uses the causative form.

Example
-------

- "Please let me borrow your eraser."
- 「消しゴムを借り**させて**下さい。」
